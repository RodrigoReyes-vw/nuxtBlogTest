#### Commercial Office: Creating a Modern Workspace

_Aired September 19 at 2 PM ET | Approved for one AIA LU, AIBC CORE, and EPP LU_

As the world changes and businesses adapt, so do the office spaces that we use[](../../../net/vectorworks/blog/more-than-an-office-the-m-groups-approach-to-corporate-offices.html). More than ever, we hear about companies trying to increase the efficiency of their existing office spaces and exploring new ways to entice employees back to work with a hybrid workspace.

Watch Kesoon Chance, senior industry specialist - interior architecture, to discover how to build an efficient model through the "fit-out" process and ways in which you can showcase these changes to your clients.

[WATCH THE INTERIOR ARCHITECTURE WEBINAR](https://university.vectorworks.net/course/view.php?id=2182).
