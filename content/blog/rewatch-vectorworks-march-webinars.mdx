## Embracing Vectorworks for Interior Design 

_Aired March 21_

Brooklyn-based design firm Claudia Giselle Design LLC., prides itself in creating luxurious interior designs that prioritize health and wellbeing.

Before switching to Vectorworks, the firm relied on multiple different drafting software. Now they’re creating 2D and 3D models as well as high-quality renderings in one designer friendly software.

Watch the webinar to learn how Claudia Giselle Design LLC. has adopted Vectorworks — to use from from conceptual designs all the way through presentations.

## A Research-Based Workflow for Lighting Design

_Aired March 29_

Jazz Hutsby, lighting designer and technician, has a wealth of experience working on theatre, live music, and corporate events. His newest passion, though? Researching the use of 3D models, assets, and digital techniques within the arts.

In this webinar, Hutsby shares how Vectorworks software offers solutions for his work on the Creative Informatics Challenge Project for the Edinburgh International Festival. Hutsby also discusses LiDAR scanning, modeling, and MVR.
