# January Webinars | Now Available On Demand

With a new year comes new learning opportunities.

By watching January's on-demand webinars, you can learn about Vectorworks’ integration with AutoTURN Online, an interior design firm's switch to Vectorworks, how to create 3D renderings of your live events, and the calculation powers of Braceworks.

Let the learning begin.

#### Driving Design Safety & Efficiency

_Aired January 18 | Approved for One APLD CEU_

Looking to up your game regarding the safety and efficiency of your projects?

Incorporating Vectorworks' intuitive site design workflows is just the beginning. Take it up a notch by utilizing AutoTURN Online, an invaluable tool that brings vehicle turn maneuvers to life. Through these simulations, you'll gain valuable insights into the functional and operational constraints of your project's vehicular circulation design.

Watch Andres Velez, a civil and transportation expert, to witness the power of AutoTURN and Vectorworks. 

[WATCH THE VECTORWORKS + AUTOTURN WEBINAR](https://university.vectorworks.net/course/view.php?id=3171).

#### Embracing Vectorworks for Interior Design

_Aired January 23_

Brooklyn-based design firm Claudia Giselle Design LLC., prides itself in creating luxurious interior designs that prioritise health and wellbeing.

Before switching to Vectorworks, the firm relied on multiple different drafting software. Now they’re creating 2D and 3D models as well as high-quality renderings in one designer friendly software.

Watch Catelyn Seifert, interior designer at Claudia Giselle Design LLC., for a webinar on how her firm has adopted Vectorworks — from conceptual designs all the way through final presentations.

[WATCH THE INTERIORS WEBINAR](https://university.vectorworks.net/course/view.php?id=2532).

You’ll learn tons of tips for 3D modeling in Vectorworks, like how textures can reduce the complexity of models, details on conceptual and free-form designs, and rendering tricks to help you present your models.
