## Door and Window Improvements

Vectorworks 2020 gives you greater control with improved[ Door and Window tools](https://university.vectorworks.net/mod/page/view.php?id=216?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=octtechroundup101719), allowing you to regulate the representation for clerestory windows above and below the cut plane. And a new Barn Door configuration in the Door tool has many options, including multiple leaf, rail, and offset configurations.
