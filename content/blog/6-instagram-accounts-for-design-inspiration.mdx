# 6 Instagram Accounts for Design Inspiration

Time for a spring refresh! To keep your feed flowering with inspiration, our team is sharing a few of our favorite accounts. Start scrolling and get ready to double-tap.

## 1\. Chris Precht ([@chrisprecht](https://www.instagram.com/chrisprecht/))

Enjoy this gallery full of fresh, new ideas that blur the lines between inside and out.

![ChrisprechtIG](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/052119_IG%20Account%20Inspiration/ChrisprechtIG.png?width=600&name=ChrisprechtIG.png)

## 2\. Zean MacFarlane ([@zeanmacfarlane](https://www.instagram.com/zeanmacfarlane/))

This carefully crafted feed boasts gorgeous architectural illustrations and diagrams.

![IMG_0989](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/052119_IG%20Account%20Inspiration/IMG_0989.png?width=600&name=IMG_0989.png)

## 3\. Lisa Congdon ([@lisacongdon](https://www.instagram.com/lisacongdon/))

Explore this artist’s vibrant work, a gallery exploding with colorful inspiration.

![IMG_0991](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/052119_IG%20Account%20Inspiration/IMG_0991.png?width=600&name=IMG_0991.png)

## 4\. Rui Vieira Oliveira Arquitecto ([@ruivieiraoliveira](https://www.instagram.com/ruivieiraoliveira/))

Two words sum up the theme of this modern design account: white and blue.

![IMG_0992](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/052119_IG%20Account%20Inspiration/IMG_0992.png?width=600&name=IMG_0992.png)

## 5\. Sasha Vithin ([@sasha\_vithin](https://www.instagram.com/sasha%5Fvithin/))

This photographer and art director’s account is pure minimalism, concentrating on clean lines and occasional color.

![IMG_0993](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/052119_IG%20Account%20Inspiration/IMG_0993.png?width=600&name=IMG_0993.png)

## 6\. Perry Kulper ([@pkulper](https://www.instagram.com/pkulper/))

If you’re looking for beautiful abstract drawings, stop here — you’ve found them.

![pkulperIG](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/052119_IG%20Account%20Inspiration/pkulperIG.png?width=600&name=pkulperIG.png)
