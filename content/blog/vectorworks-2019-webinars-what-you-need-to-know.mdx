Excited about Vectorworks 2019? Join us for a free, “What’s New” webinar and discover the latest improvements and new features.

## What’s New in Vectorworks 2019 for the AEC Industry

**![What's New ARCH](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/180914_Whats%20New%20Webinars%202019/What's%20New%20ARCH.jpg?width=770&name=What's%20New%20ARCH.jpg)**

Vectorworks Architect 2019 delivers exceptional quality, with industry-specific tools that let you adapt to challenges and design without constraints. With improved performance and added capabilities that make your BIM workflow and everyday design tasks easier to accomplish, Vectorworks Architect 2019 will help you save time and deliver exceptional results. Learn how the latest in design and BIM solutions — available to you in our newest version — will enhance your modeling process and simplify your workflows.

## What’s New in Vectorworks 2019 for the Landscape Industry

**![vw_blog_landmark_2019](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/180914_Whats%20New%20Webinars%202019/vw_blog_landmark_2019.jpg?width=770&name=vw_blog_landmark_2019.jpg)**

Vectorworks Landmark offers tools created specifically for landscape professionals to help advance their site designs. Our newest version allows users to simplify workflows, enhance the modeling process, and enjoy exceptional quality. Explore tools that will transform design, presentation, irrigation, documentation, and BIM workflows, and experience more streamlined site modeling with 3D sculpting, improvements with hardscape components, more simplified plant settings, and new capabilities for plant data sources.
