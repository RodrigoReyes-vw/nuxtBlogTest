# SILV & Vectorworks: A Partnership with Future Designers at its Core

The next generation of designers will always have our support, so we’re proud to continue our partnership with the [Stagecraft Institute of Las Vegas](https://stagecraftinstitute.com/) (SILV) for the fourth consecutive year.

![SILV_logo_color_BG](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/SILV_logo_color_BG.jpg?width=750&name=SILV_logo_color_BG.jpg)SILV is a first-of-its-kind intensive program that offers improved and efficient ways to train students in all areas of live entertainment. We support this effort by donating funds and teaching workshops on cutting-edge entertainment technology.

This year, Vectorworks Product Marketing Manager — Entertainment Frank Brault will teach two classes at SILV: a [Spotlight](http://www.vectorworks.net/spotlight?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fcontent=silv051518) class that will cover everything from basic concepts to advanced 2D and 3D techniques and a week-long course about our pre-visualization software, [Vision](http://www.espvision.com/).

**Session Dates**

[Spotlight](http://www.vectorworks.net/events/show/1084#.Wvmx8xMrLUL?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fcontent=silv051518): June 3-8, 2018

[Vision Training](http://www.vectorworks.net/events/show/1085#.WvmyKxMrLUL?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fcontent=silv051518): July 1-6, 2018

Submissions for the SILV program are ongoing, and students interested in getting involved should apply [here](http://stagecraftinstitute.com/admissions/).

“The partnership that SILV has with Vectorworks has dramatically expanded our students’ experience with digital drafting and increased their pre-visualization and console programming capabilities, making the plotting and paperwork process possible,” said Jane Childs, director at SILV. “The accomplishments achieved after two short weeks with our Vectorworks friends is phenomenal.”
