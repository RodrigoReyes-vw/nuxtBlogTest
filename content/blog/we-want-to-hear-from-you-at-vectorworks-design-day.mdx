# We Want to Hear from You at Vectorworks Design Day

Now that #Vectorworks2021 is out, here’s your chance to hear from Vectorworks leadership and users around the world about the latest innovations in the new version! 

The Vectorworks Design Day is a virtual event we’re hosting on October 6, 2020, featuring live sessions from Vectorworks CEO Dr. Biplab Sarkar, VP of Product Development Steve Johnson, and Product Marketing Director Rubina Siddiqui. They’ll discuss what’s new with an emphasis on how users can benefit from the [new and improved features](https://www.vectorworks.net/2021?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=093020designday).

There will be three live airings on October 6 to accommodate global time zones.

* Europe: 8:00 a.m. ET
* Americas: 1:00 p.m. ET
* Asia/Pacific: 7:00 p.m. ET

[![5117-2020-vectorworks-virtual-design-day-promotion-blog-2](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/200930%20Design%20Day/5117-2020-vectorworks-virtual-design-day-promotion-blog-2.jpg?width=600&name=5117-2020-vectorworks-virtual-design-day-promotion-blog-2.jpg)](https://www3.vectorworks.net/vectorworks-design-day-registration?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=button&utm%5Fcontent=093020designday)

After the live sessions, get your questions ready for a Q&A with Sarkar, Johnson, and Siddiqui. They’re ready to answer anything you’d like to know about the release — this is a great chance to get answers from the minds behind Vectorworks software!

We’re also excited to share six on-demand presentations from designers who use Vectorworks in their day-to-day work. Hear from them how Vectorworks can augment existing workflows and enable entirely new ones. Here’s the lineup:

###### **Randy Gerner, GKV Architects**

[GKV Architects](https://www.gkvarchitects.com/) is a full-service architecture and interior architecture firm from New York that has been using Vectorworks for over 25 years.

###### Nick Lawrence, A&Q Partnership (London) 

[A&Q Partnership](http://www.aqp.co.uk/) is an award-winning architecture firm who specialize in a wide range of projects, from regenerative efforts to commercial and residential.

###### Lisa McRavey & Iain Lyon, Raeburn Farquhar Bowen

[Raeburn Farquhar Bowen](http://www.raeburnfarquharbowen.com/) is a landscape architecture firm from Scotland that focuses on green and sustainable infrastructure.

###### Linette Brown, Origin Studios

[Origin Studios](http://www.originstudios.com/) from Ottowa, Canada are exhibit design experts who tell stories through innovative spaces.

###### Derek Lee, PWL Partnership

[PWL Partnership](https://www.pwlpartnership.com/) is a landscape architecture firm that has worked on Vancouver, Canada’s industrial waterfront every step of the way.

###### Ben Inskip, Siyan

[Siyan](https://siyan.co.uk/about-us) is a lighting design and rental house from the UK that has worked with big names like Amy Winehouse, ZZ Top, Coldplay, and Adele.

To top it all off, the event is **free**! All you have to do is sign up.
