###### “5 Things the New MacOS BigSur Optimized with M1 Brings to Vectorworks”

![Best Blogs 1](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/211221_Best%20Blogs%202021/Best%20Blogs%201.png?width=1440&name=Best%20Blogs%201.png)

Originally published in 2020, we updated this post with information regarding the tech advancements of [Vectorworks 2022](../../../net/vectorworks/blog/vectorworks-2022-now-available-for-download.html).

Vectorworks is the first major BIM authoring tool to run natively on Apple Silicon, in addition to the Vectorworks Graphics Module (VGM) making full use of Apple’s Metal API.

Vectorworks 2022 is also compatible with macOS Monterey, Apple’s next major operating system update.

[Click here](../../../net/vectorworks/blog/5-things-the-new-macos-big-sur-optimized-with-m1-brings-to-vectorworks.html) to check out the full story!
