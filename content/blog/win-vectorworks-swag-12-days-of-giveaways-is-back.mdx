# 12 Days of Giveaways | What You Need to Know

Get ready for the holidays with us — our 12 Days of Giveaways is back for its second year!

We kicked off the 12 Days of Giveaways last year to share holiday joy with the Vectorworks community. We’re giving away some great gifts, and you won’t want to miss a single day.

## 12 Days of Giveaways – How It Works

The giveaways run from November 30, 2022 to December 15, 2022\. Visit our [Instagram](http://instagram.com/vectorworks) each day, excluding weekends, between these dates to enter for a chance to win daily prizes. Winners will be randomly selected among those who complete all of the instructions for each day’s giveaway. Winners will be announced the following day on our Instagram Story, and winners will be notified via a direct message.

### Give Yourself the Best Chance to Win

Want to win some sweet prizes? Then you’ll want to make sure you’re following our Instagram to see each day’s giveaway instructions. To make sure you don’t miss a post, turn on post notifications.
