# From Instagram: Your Favorite Vectorworks Designs of 2021

If you’re not following Vectorworks on Instagram, you’re missing out on a ton of great content. We do giveaways, provide updates, and share great designs from around the world that were #MadeWithVectorworks.

Today, we’ll be taking a look back at some of the most popular posts we saw on our Instagram over the past 12 months. 

###### Top Architecture Design Instagram Post of 2021

_Firm/Designer:_ [AO Design Studio](https://l.instagram.com/?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.aodesignstudio.com.au%2F&e=ATOoWdooQmst7qUBbfNtiuTMrA2FWEaZ533esqS7x5vRRfpG3jPTv4lJFzCSGdpKG0tIVEVj6SGGPIPvt6Jx2WmXgU3bpXtO&s=1)

_Username:_ [@ao\_design studio](https://www.instagram.com/ao%5Fdesignstudio/)

This wonderful “Glass House” concept from AO Design Studio is a testament to the polished, modern design that you can create using [Vectorworks Architect](https://www.vectorworks.net/architect). Notice, too, the simplicity of the concept. Sometimes, less is more.

![Screen Shot 2022-01-18 at 9.17.58 AM](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/220118_Top%20Insta%20Posts/Screen%20Shot%202022-01-18%20at%209.17.58%20AM.png?width=900&name=Screen%20Shot%202022-01-18%20at%209.17.58%20AM.png)

###### Top Interior Design Instagram Post of 2021

_Firm/Designer:_ [Baldock Interiors](https://jasminebaldock.wixsite.com/home)

_Username:_ [@jbaldockinteriors](https://www.instagram.com/jbaldockinteriors/)

From a sleek design to a far more eccentric one. This interior design from Jasmine Baldock is a unique revision of the pop-up shop experience. Titled “Rainbow After the Storm,” this concept is inspired by [M·A·C Cosmetics](https://www.maccosmetics.com/).

![Screen Shot 2022-01-18 at 9.21.28 AM](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/220118_Top%20Insta%20Posts/Screen%20Shot%202022-01-18%20at%209.21.28%20AM.png?width=900&name=Screen%20Shot%202022-01-18%20at%209.21.28%20AM.png)

###### **Top Landscape Design Instagram Post of 2021**

_Firm/Designer:_ [TIM Exclusive Gardens](https://www.tim-exclusivegardens.nl/)

_Username:_ [@timexclusivegardens.nl](https://www.instagram.com/timexclusivegardens.nl/)

Great design has the ability to deeply affect your mood. In this case, TIM Exclusive Gardens used [Vectorworks Landmark](https://www.vectorworks.net/landmark) to create an outdoor concept that’s sure to fill you with peaceful tranquility. We certainly wouldn’t mind spending a summer evening here!

![Screen Shot 2022-01-18 at 9.22.38 AM](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/220118_Top%20Insta%20Posts/Screen%20Shot%202022-01-18%20at%209.22.38%20AM.png?width=958&name=Screen%20Shot%202022-01-18%20at%209.22.38%20AM.png)

###### Top Entertainment Design Instagram Post of 2021

_Firm/Designer:_ [Blake Addington Designs](https://l.instagram.com/?u=https%3A%2F%2Flinktr.ee%2FBlakeAddingtonDesigns&e=ATP6quX%5FFAtJrqc41dG2RJ7KX-S41blK0Wq2D9IU%5FRpQhhNbWkSbIy8z7bogF4-ijxKXE5SGBCJON8iOBXIitdwkhjwJJqgy&s=1)

_Username:_ [@blakeaddingtondesigns](https://www.instagram.com/blakeaddingtondesigns/)

Lastly, let’s turn back time to check out Blake Addington’s lighting design for Gramatik’s show at the iconic Red Rocks Amphitheater. Addington created the design using the industry-leading [Vectorworks Spotlight](https://www.vectorworks.net/en-US/spotlight). Turn up the great design!
