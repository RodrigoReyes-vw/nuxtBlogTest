Our May Tech Roundup is all about bringing your vision into focus. This month, our Vectorworks experts have compiled tips that will help you add believable lighting to your project, as well as fix a pixelated viewport. 

## Add a Heliodon Object to Your File

If you're looking for an all-in-one tool to set sun positions and create solar animations for your next project, this tech tip has exactly what you need. Dylan Rinker, senior technical support specialist, breaks down how Heliodon objects bring real-world lighting to your designs, walking you through the process from dropping one into a file to customizing your lighting and generating accurate shadows.
