# April Webinars | Now Available On Demand

Looking for ways to galvanize your design process? Our April webinars will give you the tips and tricks you’re searching for.

These webinars cover [Passive House](https://passivehouse-international.org/index.php?page%5Fid=150), leveraging [Unreal Engine](https://www.unrealengine.com/?utm%5Fsource=GoogleSearch&utm%5Fmedium=Performance&utm%5Fcampaign=%7Bcampaigname%7D&utm%5Fid=17086214833&sub%5Fcampaign=&utm%5Fcontent=&utm%5Fterm=unreal%20engine), and adopting a BIM process.

Rewatch your industry’s webinar below:

#### Workflows for Passive House & Deep Retrofits

_Aired April 18_ 

Deep connections between homeowners and their homes begin with a comprehensive design workflow. And, integrating energy modeling into the early stages of the design process offers clients insight on how high-performance buildings will impact their lives.

In this webinar, [Habit Studio](https://www.habitstudio.ca/), an architecture and residential design firm that’s a leading authority in sustainable home design, shared the workflows and tools that help them achieve their sustainability goals.

This webinar is also approved for one AIA LU/HSW, AIBC CORE, AAA LU, and EPP LU.

#### Ureal Engine & Vectorworks for Live Events

_Aired April 19_ 

Patrick Wambold, Senior Solution Architect at [Epic Games](https://store.epicgames.com/), has worked for the likes of Fortnite, Rocket League, Coachella, Tomorrowland, and the Super Bowl.

In this informative webinar, Wambold dissected a portion of the 2022 Fortnite Championship Series Invitational to explore using Vectorworks and Unreal Engine for a live esports event. He also covered exporting from Vectorworks using the GDTF, MVR, and Datasmith file formats to import designs into Unreal Engine and MA3.

#### Switching Your Firm to a BIM Workflow & Mindset 

_Aired April 20_ 

Changing to new software can be intimidating, but the right support and advice can help ease onboarding team members during the transition. This webinar, hosted by Nick Fullmer, design-build business owner and BIM implementation and use expert, shared tips and strategies for adopting a smarter, data-driven workflow for your landscape architecture or design practice.

The smoother the transition to BIM, the sooner your firm can design like never before.

This webinar is also approved for one LA CES PDH and APLD CEU.
