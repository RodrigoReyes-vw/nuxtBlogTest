**New Content Libraries Available**

Over 400 different content libraries that were only accessible to Vectorworks Service Select members are now available for download through the Resource Manager in Vectorworks 2019\. Choose from top industry brands such as Techo Bloc, Proven Winners, Armitage Shanks, Ideal Standard, Meyer Sound, Kling and Freitag, Allen and Heath, Stageline, Altman, Apollo, Chauvet, Clay Paky, City Theatrical, ETC, High End, PRG, Martin, Robe, Toshiba, Varilite, Prolyte, Royal Botania, and more.
