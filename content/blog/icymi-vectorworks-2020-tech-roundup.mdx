## List browser direct editing

Software interfaces with a lot of options can become unwieldy to users. With Vectorworks 2020, eliminate frustrations by [directly editing fields in a list browser](https://university.vectorworks.net/mod/page/view.php?id=235?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=techroundup091919), such as the Navigation palette or the Organization dialog.

## Video Camera Object

A new [Video Camera object](https://university.vectorworks.net/mod/page/view.php?id=286?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=techroundup091919) in [Vectorworks Spotlight](https://www.vectorworks.net/en-US/spotlight?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=techroundup091919) helps you plan real-world camera placement.

## [Hardscape Alignment](https://university.vectorworks.net/mod/page/view.php?id=218?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=techroundup091919)

With customizable approaches to modeling hardscapes, use the 3D geometry of other features [to align and conform hardscape objects](https://university.vectorworks.net/mod/page/view.php?id=218?utm%5Fcampaign=blog&utm%5Fmedium=planetvectorworks&utm%5Fsource=intext&utm%5Fcontent=techroundup091919) to 3D polygons, grade objects, roadways, or other hardscapes. It’s now possible to design paved surfaces to drain properly and align to other paved surfaces — remaining accurately modeled and perfectly controlled.
