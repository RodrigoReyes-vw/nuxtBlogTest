# January Tech Roundup: Tips from Industry Experts

For the first tech roundup of 2021, hear from industry and training experts for your monthly dose of tech tips.

## Hatching Workflows

**![Hatching Workflows](https://blog.vectorworks.net/hs-fs/hubfs/Blog%20Images/210128_Tech%20Roundup/Hatching%20Workflows.jpg?width=600&name=Hatching%20Workflows.jpg)**

Learn how you can improve your Hatching workflows from Vectorworks Architect Industry Specialist Luka Stefanovic. Hatching is an essential part of technical drawings employed by architects to create beautiful designs, represent materials, or build elements. Find out how to create a link between textures and hatches and use parametric settings to automatically align the textures with hatches.
