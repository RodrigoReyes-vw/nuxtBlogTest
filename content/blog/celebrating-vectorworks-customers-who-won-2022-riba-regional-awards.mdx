# Celebrating Vectorworks Customers Who Won 2022 RIBA Regional Awards

The Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA), a global professional membership body, has announced the 2022 Regional Awards winning projects, and many of them were designed using Vectorworks!

This is exciting news, particularly because RIBA has posted images and write ups about each of the projects. You’ll find links to learn more about them below.

The awards are based on geographical location and are [judged and presented locally by esteemed jurors](https://www.architecture.com/knowledge-and-resources/knowledge-landing-page/2022-regional-award-juries). Here are the regions as well as the winning firms:

## East

* Nissen Richards - [Sutton Hoo](https://www.architecture.com/awards-and-competitions-landing-page/awards/riba-regional-awards/riba-east-award-winners/2022/sutton-hoo)
* 31/44 Architects - [BlueSky Barn](https://www.architecture.com/awards-and-competitions-landing-page/awards/riba-regional-awards/riba-east-award-winners/2022/bluesky-barn)
* Butcher Bayley Architects - [Copper House](https://www.architecture.com/awards-and-competitions-landing-page/awards/riba-regional-awards/riba-east-award-winners/2022/copper-house)
* Niall McLaughlin Architects - [Magdalene College Library](https://www.architecture.com/awards-and-competitions-landing-page/awards/riba-regional-awards/riba-east-award-winners/2022/magdalene-college-library)
* Mole Architects - [Mere House](https://www.architecture.com/awards-and-competitions-landing-page/awards/riba-regional-awards/riba-east-award-winners/2022/Mere-House) and [Friars](https://www.architecture.com/awards-and-competitions-landing-page/awards/riba-regional-awards/riba-east-award-winners/2022/friars)
